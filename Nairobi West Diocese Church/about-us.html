About Us

Who We Are

The Nairobi West Diocese is committed to spreading the Gospel and serving the community through various programs and services. Established in 2002, it has grown to include several churches across Kenya. The diocese focuses on youth engagement, community service, and spiritual growth. Our mission is to create a supportive environment where individuals can come together to worship, learn, and grow in their faith. We offer a range of services including Sunday School, youth programs, and community outreach initiatives. Join us at one of our services or community events and become a part of our vibrant and growing community.

Church Activities

Our church participates in various activities, inclusive of:

  • Bible studies
  • Men’s teachings
  • Women’s teachings
  • Hospital and home visitations to the sick and those in need
  • Christmas Carols and Cake event
  • Youths teachings
  • Prayer meetings
  • Sunday School teachings
